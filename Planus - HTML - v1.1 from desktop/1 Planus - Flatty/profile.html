Profile


Hello, I'm
Jacqueline Quenneville

I am an Emily Carr alumni and my background is in Industrial Design.
What I loved about Industrial design was the ability to create products that have a meaningful impact and improve people’s lives. But what I didn’t love about Industrial design was, at it’s core it creates things, it creates junk. It’s unsustainable.
Because I am a life-long learner and a life-long nerd, making the switch to web seemed like a natural progression. In web design, I see the opportunity to take what I love about Industrial design—making people’s lives better through designed experiences—and combine it with my love of technology to create amazing user experiences
